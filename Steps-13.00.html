Question:  How to use a Line Chart to Determine Revenue Growth By Year

 

Below we will add the Combo Chart to answer question follow below.


Add Dimension “OrigYear” on the Combo chart.


 

Add Measure “Yearly Revenue” for the combo Chart as 1st Measure value to show the growth in bars.

 


 

Add the 2nd Measure Value “Yearly Revenue” on the Combo Chart to show the growth in Lines.


 

Add Combo Chart properties to give more description for the Chart. We will Add Show Data Points and Point Labels on Appearance Presentation Tab. 


 

Now the Chart is displaying with all it’s configuration. We will Add title “Revenue Growth by Year” for our chart to give the summary of the Chart.
